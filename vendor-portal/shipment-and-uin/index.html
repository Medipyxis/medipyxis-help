Shipments and UINs (replaced by Manage orders)

how-to ⏱ 1 min read Vendor Updated 2026-07-08

This page has been replaced. Vendors do not receive shipments or assign UINs — you ship an order and record its tracking number; units are received into inventory on the clinic side.

Go to Manage orders for the real flow: accept → in progress → ship (with tracking) → delivered.

For an overview of the portal, see the Vendor Portal Guide.
