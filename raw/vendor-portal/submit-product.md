# Manage your product catalog (SKUs)

Maintain the products your company offers to clinics from the **Catalog** page of the vendor portal (`/vendor/catalog`). You own your SKUs directly — adding or updating one takes effect without a separate practice approval step.

## Before you start

- You are signed in to the vendor portal (`vendor_admin` or `vendor_user`). See [Sign in and manage staff](./onboarding-your-company.md).

## Add or update a SKU

1. Open **Catalog**.
2. Click **New SKU** (or open an existing SKU to edit it).
3. Enter the product details — name, your SKU code, modality, HCPCS code where applicable, and handling attributes (for example cold-chain or consignable flags).
4. **Save.** The SKU is written to your catalog and is available to the clinics you serve.

<Note>
The vendor catalog you manage here is your company's own SKU list. It is separate from a clinic's internal inventory Product Catalog. There is no 510(k)/IFU upload or "pending approval" gate in the portal — you maintain your SKUs directly.
</Note>
