# Respond to an IVR (replaced by Manage orders)

This page has been replaced. The vendor portal does not have an "IVR response" workflow — vendors work the orders that clinics place with them.

**Go to [Manage orders](./manage-orders.md)** to accept an order, mark it in progress, ship it with a tracking number, and track it to delivery.

For an overview of the portal, see the [Vendor Portal Guide](../vendor-portal/).
