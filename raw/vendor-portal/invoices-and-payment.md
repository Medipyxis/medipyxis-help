# Send invoices

Submit invoices to the clinics you supply from the **Invoices** page of the vendor portal (`/vendor/invoices`). In Medipyxis, the vendor **sends** the invoice; the clinic's finance team reconciles and pays it on their side.

## Before you start

- You are signed in to the vendor portal (`vendor_admin` or `vendor_user`). See [Sign in and manage staff](./onboarding-your-company.md).
- The order the invoice covers has shipped — see [Manage orders](./manage-orders.md).

## Send an invoice

1. Open **Invoices**. The list shows the invoices you've submitted and their status.
2. Create a new invoice, tie it to the relevant order/clinic, and enter the amount and line detail.
3. Submit it. The clinic's finance team picks it up in their Accounts Payable workspace, where they match, approve, and pay it.

<Note>
Vendors **send** invoices; they do not review or approve them. Matching, disputes, and payment happen on the clinic side (their Accounts Payable module). Watch your Invoices list for status changes, and follow up with your clinic contact on anything unpaid.
</Note>
