# Sign in and manage staff

The vendor portal is its own sign-in at `/vendor`, separate from the clinical app. Your company has two roles: **vendor_admin** (full access, including staff management) and **vendor_user** (everything except Staff).

## Accept your invitation and sign in

1. Your company's `vendor_admin` (or the clinic that set your company up) invites you by email. Open the invite and follow the accept link to set your password.
2. Go to `/vendor` and sign in. You land on the vendor **Home** page.
3. The portal navigation gives you **Home**, **Orders**, **Use Events**, **Catalog**, **Invoices**, and **Clinics**. **Staff** appears only for `vendor_admin`.

## Manage staff (vendor_admin only)

1. Open **Staff**.
2. Invite a colleague by email and assign them **vendor_admin** or **vendor_user**.
3. `vendor_user` accounts can work orders, catalog, invoices, use events, and clinics, but cannot open **Staff** — only a `vendor_admin` manages team access.

<Note>
Vendor accounts are managed inside the vendor portal's own **Staff** page — not through the clinic's HR & Compliance. A clinic never sees or manages your internal staff list.
</Note>
